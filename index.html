About Me

A little bit about me

As a Lead Data Analyst, I have got over 2 years of practical experience in the field of data analytics.I took on a career switch in 2021 from a MSc in Research Methods in Psychology to a career in data, and I've loved my decision! Currently, I am contracted to an international Magic Circle law firm as a Business Intelligence Developer and Business Analyst, working daily with Power BI, SQL and migration of data and reports as part of a programme of delivery. Previously, I was contracted to a multinational leader for transport and logistics, where I was able to utilise my skills in Excel and Power BI to help the business make data-driven decisions. I am passionate about personal and professional self-development, continously working to improve my technical skills in SQL, Power BI and other technical skills which spark my interest. I was able to successfuly pursue my dream career in data and technology, and I have a drive for giving back to the community and advocating for an increase in women pursuing these roles. In December 2022, I won the Rising Star UK award for the Globant Women that Build Awards, and have since been nominated for another Rising Star award. In her spare time, I am a Medium writer and author of the 'From A to SQL' series, a Trainee Instructor for Code First Girls, a Trustee for EmployabilityUK and my hobbies include reading, listening to podcasts and taking walks.
